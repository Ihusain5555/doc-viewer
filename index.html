    LIVE DOCUMENT VIEWER

    View your Google Docs, live.

    1. In Google Docs, click Share → General access → “Anyone with the link” and set it to Viewer.
    2. Copy the link, paste it in the box on the left, and click Add.
    3. Click a doc to read it live, or make a fill — a private editable copy you can type into, keep, and download. You can make as many fills per doc as you want; your real doc stays untouched.

    Sharing this app with friends? They just open the same link and add their own docs — or send them a Back up file to load your whole set of docs and fills at once.
